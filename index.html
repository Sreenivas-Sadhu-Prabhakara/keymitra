How to type the sounds

keymitra reads your letters left to right, always taking the longest match first, so kh, ch, sh, aa and ai are read as single sounds.

    Consonant tips

    • kh gh ch chh jh th dh ph bh — aspirated pairs.
    • T Th D Dh N S — capitals give the retroflex ट ठ ड ढ ण ष.
    • sh = श / ಶ, Sh = ष / ಷ, ~n ~N = ञ ङ.
    • Two consonants in a row join as a cluster: satya → सत्य.

      A typing aid, not a certified transliteration standard. keymitra follows a practical ITRANS-style scheme; Devanagari and Kannada are verified against exact Unicode, while Tamil and Telugu are marked beta and can collapse sounds their scripts do not encode. Rendering needs the script's font on your device—keymitra ships none. Always proofread important text.
